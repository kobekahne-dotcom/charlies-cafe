Our Story

Charlie's began in 1976, when John and Charlotte Spear took over a little Enumclaw café and named it after Charlotte — known to everyone as "Charlie." Three generations later it's still in the family: Charlie's granddaughter Autumn and her husband Ben run it today, serving the same home-cooked breakfast, bottomless coffee, and warm welcome that's fed the plateau for half a century. From our family to yours — pull up a chair.
